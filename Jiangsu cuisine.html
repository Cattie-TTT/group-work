Vince tofu

Originated in Huai 'an and Yangzhou, Jiangsu Province, Wensi Tofu is a traditional specialty dish in Jiangsu Province, belonging to the Jiangsu cuisine - Huaiyang cuisine. This dish has a long history, and its ingredients are extremely strict. With fine cutting skills, it is soft, tender and mellow, and melts in your mouth

Yangzhou fried rice

Yangzhou fried rice, also known as Yangzhou egg-fried rice, is a famous traditional dish in Yangzhou, Jiangsu province. It belongs to Huaiyang cuisine. Its main ingredients are rice, ham, egg, shrimp, etc. Yangzhou fried rice is careful in selecting ingredients, making, processing and color matching.

Braised Shredded Chicken with Ham and Dried Tofu

Dastewed Dry silk, also known as "boiled dry silk with chicken sauce", "boiled dry silk with chicken fire", formerly known as "Nine silk Soup", belongs to Huaiyang cuisine. It is one of the classic dishes of Huaiyang, and also one of the representative dishes of Huaiyang cuisine known for its exquisite knife skills and heat.

Dongpo Braised Pork

Dongpo pork is also known as boiled pork and braised pork in brown sauce. It is a famous traditional dish of han nationality in jiangnan region, belonging to Zhejiang cuisine and Sichuan cuisine, with pork as the main ingredient.
